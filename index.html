Stephen and Sue started to establish this four hectare orchard located on Dunstan Road, close to Clyde in Central Otago in 1996.

The orchard was originally planted in Apricot trees which have been removed and replaced with Walnut, Hazelnut and a small planting of Almonds and Pinenut trees. Steve and Sue found the cold winters and dry summers of Central Otago perfect for growing nuts as this combination reduced the chance of disease and pests, and as a result a very clean, full of flavor, high quality product is produced.

All the nuts they sell are grown and processed on their property to ensure the product is of exceptional quality, divine flavors and always guaranteed to be fresh.
